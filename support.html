PocketGPT Support

PocketGPT is an iPhone app for local AI chat. Download a model once, then chat with it on device without accounts, analytics, ads, telemetry, cloud sync, or cloud inference.

PocketGPT is currently available for iPhone. iPad support may be added in a future release.

Privacy

See the PocketGPT Privacy Policy. Your chats, prompt profiles, attachments, settings, and downloaded models stay on your device.

Known limitations

  • AI output can be inaccurate, incomplete, biased, unsafe, or offensive.
  • AI output is not professional advice.
  • Models are large downloads and may require newer iPhones for good performance.
  • Some model hosts may be unavailable in some regions or networks.
  • Voice input requires Apple on-device speech recognition support for the current device and language.
