PocketGPT Privacy Policy

Effective date: 07 June 2026

App: PocketGPT for iPhone on iOS

Provider / data controller: Valeriy Volchetskiy

Contact: theloop.studio.office@gmail.com

PocketGPT is currently released as an iPhone-only app. iPad support may be added in a future release.

1. Summary

PocketGPT runs AI chat models locally on your device. Your chats, prompt profiles, attachments, settings, generated model output, and downloaded model files are stored on your device and are not sent to PocketGPT or to any third-party server for AI processing.

PocketGPT does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, accounts, cloud sync, cloud inference, or telemetry. The only network connection built into PocketGPT is a user-initiated model download from the model host.

2. Data Stored On Your Device

PocketGPT stores the following data locally so the app can work. All of it stays on your device and none of it is sent to PocketGPT:

  • Chat messages and chat history — for local AI chat.
  • Generated model output — for conversation history and local app use.
  • Prompt profiles and quick actions — reusable local instructions.
  • Attachments you import — for local text extraction, OCR, or model input.
  • App settings — model, appearance, and generation settings.
  • Downloaded model files — stored on device after download for local AI inference.

PocketGPT does not receive, collect, sell, share, or store this data.

Depending on your iOS settings, locally stored app data may be included in device backups managed by Apple, such as iCloud or encrypted computer backups. PocketGPT does not control Apple backup services. You can manage device backup settings in iOS Settings.

3. Network Connections

3.1 Model Downloads

When you choose to download a model, PocketGPT downloads model files over HTTPS from the model host currently listed in the app catalog, such as Hugging Face (huggingface.co). The download request is a standard file request for the model artifacts. Your chats, prompts, attachments, settings, generated output, and local app content are not sent as part of a model download.

The model host may process standard technical request information, such as IP address, requested URL, user agent, and request time, under its own privacy policy. Hugging Face's privacy policy is available at huggingface.co/privacy.

3.2 Voice Input

If you use voice input, PocketGPT uses Apple's Speech framework with on-device recognition only. Audio is transcribed locally on your device and is not sent to PocketGPT or to Apple's servers by PocketGPT. On devices or languages that do not support on-device recognition, voice input is disabled and you can still type your message.

4. Permissions

PocketGPT asks for permissions only when you use the related feature:

  • Camera — to capture images you choose to attach for local analysis.
  • Photo Library — to attach images you choose for local analysis.
  • Microphone and Speech Recognition — to transcribe voice input locally on your device.
  • Files — to import documents, PDFs, text files, and other files you choose for local analysis.

You can change or revoke these permissions in iOS Settings.

5. What PocketGPT Does Not Do

PocketGPT does not:

  • collect personal data;
  • sell or share personal data;
  • track you across apps or websites;
  • use third-party analytics, advertising, or telemetry SDKs;
  • require an account, login, or sign-up;
  • sync your chats, attachments, settings, or models to a cloud service;
  • send your chats, prompts, attachments, generated output, or model output to any cloud inference service.

6. Children's Privacy

PocketGPT is not directed to children. Because PocketGPT provides access to general-purpose AI models that can produce inaccurate or inappropriate output, the recommended App Store age rating for PocketGPT is 17+.

PocketGPT does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Deleting Data

Because PocketGPT stores user content locally, you control deletion on your device:

  • delete individual chats and prompt profiles inside the app;
  • delete downloaded models inside Settings / Models;
  • delete PocketGPT from your device to remove locally stored app data.

If you have questions, contact the provider at theloop.studio.office@gmail.com.
